Background colors should be limited to the colors provided in the template.

![Examples of slides using the background colors (black, white, blue, purple, yellow) from the template](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/55435593-0a97ab00-554f-11e9-83b1-74aa14dd7411.png)

#### Usage guidelines

<DoDontContainer>
  <Dont>
    <img
      alt="slide with octocat logo and a red to yellow to purple radial gradient behind it"
      src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/54648119-40726500-4a62-11e9-95cc-f2798867dfd6.png"
    />
    <Caption>Don't use gradients or colors outside the provided template</Caption>
  </Dont>
  <Dont>
    <img
      alt="slide with octocat logo on top of a star tiled background"
      src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/54648132-4d8f5400-4a62-11e9-9445-25016320e1fd.png"
    />
    <Caption>Don't use tiled images</Caption>
  </Dont>
</DoDontContainer>

## Background images

![Section Title - Hubot](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/55435514-e0de8400-554e-11e9-9376-42cebd3c1c83.png)

Background images should be used _only_ to support, not to distract the viewers. Lower the opacity of a background image to maintain readability.

<DoDontContainer>
  <Do>
    <img
      alt="slide with the background dimmed with black to highlight the white text"
      src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/54647253-06539400-4a5f-11e9-8315-f14eefee699f.png"
    />
    <Caption>Background images should be dimmed to 25% opacity</Caption>
  </Do>
  <Dont>
    <img
      width="500"
      alt="slide without dimming over a background image and unreadable white text"
      src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/54647119-a9f07480-4a5e-11e9-93c1-29d6116b8492.png"
    />
    <Caption>Don't use busy background images when paired with text</Caption>
  </Dont>
</DoDontContainer>
