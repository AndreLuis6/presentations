---
title: Footer
---

In the Primer presentation templates, you can control all of the footer information from one source of truth.

Footer elements can be updated globally within a presentation from the `Variables` slide which is located in the layout templates of the presentation format in use. Items that can be controlled are the `Username`, `Presentation Name`, and `Presentation Note`.

## Accessing the layout template (Keynote)

To access the layout template you can do one of the following:

|                                      Step                                       |                                                                       Example                                                                        |
| :-----------------------------------------------------------------------------: | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------: |
|       Click on **View > Edit Master Slides** in the menu bar at the top.        | ![Click View > Edit Master Slides in menu bar](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/56168669-b2ba6480-5f90-11e9-9689-f6827e60a8de.png) |
| Click on **Edit Master Slide** in the Inspector located on the right-hand side. |     ![Edit Master Slide in the Inspector](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/56168688-bea62680-5f90-11e9-835f-38d36bbe67b7.png)      |

## Editing the variables

|                                                                          Step                                                                           |                                                                 Example                                                                 |
| :-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------: | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------: |
| 1. Select the **Variables** slide on the **Navigator\*** located on the left-hand side.<br />\*If you do not see this menu, click **View > Navigator**. |   ![Select the Variables slide](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/56168721-d54c7d80-5f90-11e9-88a0-2ef672b83c1b.png)   |
|                     2. Edit the content with your own information. If you'd like to leave a section blank, just add an empty space.                     |      ![The Variables slide](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/57877127-e5dc5780-77cb-11e9-923e-65c2823d70b8.png)       |
|                                                                 Demo: Editing variables                                                                 | ![Editing the variables is easy!](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/57877220-21772180-77cc-11e9-9488-a02f156cfb0b.png) |

![Changes show instantly in the slides in your presentation](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/10384315/57877221-21772180-77cc-11e9-92b4-56d7599511dc.png)

Any changes to the variables can be seen instantly in both the layout templates and the presentation slides.
